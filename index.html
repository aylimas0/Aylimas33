Welcome

Aylimas Beach & Resort is a welcoming 3 star+ hotel located facing the sea within a verdant palm grove, contrasting with the simplicity and whiteness of its typical architecture. With 420 well-appointed rooms, the hotel is the perfect destination for both couples and families. Each room category such as Standard garden or sea view, quadruple and family superior rooms, features air conditioning or heating, internet WIFI, safe, telephone, satellite TV, and bathroom with bath or shower.

Our Services

Entertainment

1 outdoor pool with slides, 1 children’s pool, 1 indoor pool, and 2 sea water pools at the private beach.

Wellness

Thalassotherapy center with a range of relaxing treatments, massages, sauna, hammam, and more to recharge your body and soul.

Activities

Tennis court, darts, aerobics, yoga, karaoke, nightclub, kids club, children’s play area, daytime and evening shows, entertainment programs, and a thalassotherapy center for relaxation and well-being.
