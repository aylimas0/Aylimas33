About Us

Aylimas Beach & Resort is a 3‑star+ beachfront paradise nestled in palm groves. Ideal for couples, families, and dreamers, our 420 rooms offer sea views, comfort, and modern amenities.

Our Services

Restaurants & Bars

Our hotel features a central restaurant offering a wide variety in a welcoming atmosphere and 2 à la carte restaurants that provide an elevated dining experience. Bars include the main lobby bar, pool bar, and beach bar — all designed to enhance your stay with great service and a wide variety of drinks.

Entertainment Areas

We provide a variety of entertainment areas including outdoor pools with slides, indoor pools, pools for children, and 2 seawater pools located on our private beach.

Activities & Spa

Activities include tennis, darts, aerobic, yoga, karaoke, nightclub, and a kids club. Our spa and thalassotherapy center is designed to promote relaxation and well-being with sauna, hammam, and massages.
